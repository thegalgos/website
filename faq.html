What is your return and refund policy?

We gladly accept returns for any of the following reasons:

  • We sent the incorrect NFT
  • Your purchase was confirmed on-chain but you did not receive your NFT

NOTE: If you bought a Galgo and used an exchange address as your receiving address then it is unfortunately not possible to retrieve your NFT.

What levels of rarity exist within The Galgos?

Levels of Rarity Include:

  • Common: 55+ Produced
  • Uncommon: 21-34 Produced
  • Rare: 8-13 Produced
  • Ultra-Rare: 3-5 Produced
  • Epic: 1-2 Produced

Note: Every Galgo pack consists of different hand drawn characters with varying rarities. Some of the Epic and Ultra-Rare will be sold at auction on it's respective reveal date. Follow us on Social Media to keep up-to-date.
